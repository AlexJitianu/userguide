How to Perform Common Review Tasks

Add a comment:

  1. Make a selection in the document content.
  2. Tap or click the Add comment button from Review toolbar.
  3. Input a comment in the displayed dialog box. Tap or click Comment to commit it into the document.
    Note: To modify one of your comments, select the comment and tap or click the Change button.
  4. The commented text is highlighted.

See the list of all comments or tracked changes from your document:

  1. Tap or click the Review Panel tab from the upper right side corner (or swipe from right to left on mobile devices). A side panel is displayed with all the comments and tracked changes.
  2. Tap or click one of the comments or tracked changes to see it highlighted in the document area.
  3. Hover over or select a particular comment to display the Remove and Change actions. For the tracked changes, the list of available actions are: Accept, Reject, and Comment.

Navigate through the comments or tracked changes:

  1. Use the left and right arrow buttons () from the Review toolbar to navigate between comments or tracked changes.
  2. The current comment or tracked change is highlighted in the document. Also, it is displayed in the right-side Review panel.
